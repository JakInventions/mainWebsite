Brick Drop

Brick Drop is a game in which you play as a square moving side to side that has to dodge bricks. To stop moving simply tap and hold as long as you would like to remain still. Brick Drop is available on the google play store, and amazon app store.

Elemental

Elemental is a simple open-source life counter for Magic the Gathering. It's available on the google play store, and has a web version for ios users.

Elemental Website

FreeXT

FreeXT is an open-source web based notes app. It's available on the google play store, and amazon app store. FreeXT is also available for windows, linux, and has a web version.
